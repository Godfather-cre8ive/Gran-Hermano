Academics

A Comprehensive, Balanced Curriculum

Our curriculum blends the Nigerian National Curriculum with Cambridge International standards, providing a rich and balanced education for every child.

Crèche & Early Years

Focus on sensory play, motor skill development, social interaction and early language development in a warm, nurturing environment.

Nursery Programme

Phonics, numeracy, arts and crafts, storytelling and structured play designed to build a solid foundation for formal learning.

Primary Education

Core subjects including English, Mathematics, Basic Science, Social Studies, Civic Education, Fine Art, Music and Physical Education.

Junior Secondary

JSS 1–3 offering all core junior secondary subjects, with guided options in arts, sciences, and commercial streams.

Senior Secondary / Cambridge

SS1–SS3 with Cambridge IGCSE and WAEC pathways. Students receive expert coaching for both local and international examinations.

Digital & ICT Learning

Coding, computer science, digital literacy, and technology-driven learning integrated across all levels from Primary 1 upward.
