Entrance Examination

All prospective students applying for Primary 1 and above are required to sit for an entrance examination. This helps us assess each child's current academic level so we can place them appropriately.

The examination is designed to be fair, age-appropriate and stress-free. It focuses on English Language, Mathematics and General Knowledge.

📅 Examination Dates

📝 What to Expect

  • English Language (reading, writing, comprehension)
  • Mathematics (numeracy and problem-solving)
  • General Knowledge & Reasoning
  • Oral assessment for younger children
