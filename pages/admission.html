How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Visit our admissions office or complete the online application form on our website. A non-refundable form fee applies.

2

Sit for Entrance Examination

All prospective students (except crèche and nursery) must sit for our entrance examination. Exam dates are published on our website.

3

Interview

Successful applicants will be invited for an interview with parents/guardians and the school's admissions panel.

4

Offer Letter & Acceptance

Successful candidates receive an offer letter. Parents must confirm acceptance and pay the acceptance fee within the stipulated period.

5

Resumption & Enrolment

Newly admitted students resume on the date specified in their offer letter. All school kit and documentation must be completed before resumption.

📋 Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Most recent school report/result
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Immunisation card (for Crèche/Nursery)
  • Medical fitness report
  • Parent/guardian valid ID

💰 Fee Information

  • Application form fee: Available on request
  • Acceptance fee: Available on request
  • School fees: Payable termly
  • For full fee schedule, contact the school directly
