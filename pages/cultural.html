GHA Cultural Day

Cultural Day is one of the most colourful and anticipated events in the Gran Hermano Academy calendar. Students showcase the rich diversity of Nigerian and African culture through traditional attire, music, dance, food and drama performances.

The event brings together students, parents, staff and invited guests to celebrate heritage, unity and cultural pride. It is an opportunity for students to express creativity, develop confidence and appreciate the beauty of their roots.
