Sports & Physical Development

At Gran Hermano Academy, we believe that a well-rounded education includes physical development. Our sports programme develops teamwork, discipline, healthy competition and physical fitness in every student.

We participate in inter-house sports competitions, local and state-level athletics events, and encourage every student to find a sport they love.

🏅 Sports Offered

  • Basketball
  • Table Tennis
  • Athletics & Track Events
  • Volleyball
  • Swimming
  • Chess
