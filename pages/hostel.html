A Home Away From Home

Our student hostels are designed to provide maximum comfort, safety and a nurturing environment where students feel truly at home. We take great pride in the quality of our boarding facilities.

Separate hostels are provided for male and female students, each supervised by experienced houseparents who ensure the wellbeing of all boarders round the clock.

🏠 Hostel Features

  • Separate male and female dormitories
  • 24/7 supervision by trained houseparents
  • Comfortable beds, wardrobes & study areas
  • Clean bathrooms and sanitation facilities
  • Nutritious meals served daily (dining hall)
  • Structured evening prep (supervised study)
  • Recreation room and outdoor spaces
  • CCTV security coverage
