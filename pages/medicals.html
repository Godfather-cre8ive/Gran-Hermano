Student Medical Care

The health of our students is our top priority. Gran Hermano Academy maintains a fully equipped school medical unit staffed by trained medical personnel available during school hours.

We carry out regular medical check-ups, maintain detailed health records for each student, and ensure prompt first-aid response to any medical situation on campus. Parents are immediately notified of any health concerns.

🏥 Our Medical Services Include

  • On-site medical unit with qualified staff
  • Regular health screenings and check-ups
  • First aid and emergency response
  • Health education and hygiene promotion
  • Linkage with nearby hospitals for specialist care
  • Individual student health record management
