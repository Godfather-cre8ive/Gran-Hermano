Our Examination Hall

Gran Hermano Academy's examination hall is a spacious, well-ventilated and purpose-built facility designed to provide the ideal environment for students during examinations.

The hall is equipped with individual examination desks, proper lighting, air-conditioning and strict invigilation systems to ensure fairness and integrity in every examination.

🏛 Hall Specifications

  • Seating capacity: 200+ students
  • Full air-conditioning
  • Individual examination carrels
  • CCTV monitoring during exams
  • Dedicated entrance/exit procedures
  • Approved for WAEC, NECO & Cambridge exams
